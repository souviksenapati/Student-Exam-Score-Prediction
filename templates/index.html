About the Project

The Student Score Predictor is an intelligent web application that revolutionizes academic performance forecasting by analyzing multiple student factors including study hours, attendance rates, previous academic scores, extracurricular participation, sleep patterns, and health conditions.

Built with Flask as the backend framework, this project integrates cutting-edge machine learning models including LightGBM and XGBoost to deliver highly accurate score predictions. The platform stands out through its implementation of SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) technology, providing complete model transparency by identifying the top factors influencing each prediction.

Beyond simple predictions, the system generates personalized, data-driven recommendations such as "Increase study hours by 2 hours" or "Improve attendance by 15%" - empowering students with actionable insights to enhance their academic performance.

The application features a responsive design with real-time validation, ensuring total daily hours don't exceed 24 hours, and offers dual-model support for comparative analysis. This makes it an ideal tool for educational institutions, tutors, and students seeking evidence-based academic improvement strategies.

Designed for CPU-based deployment with lightweight architecture, the platform combines high performance, accuracy, and interpretability to support data-driven educational decision making.
